Haku

Haku is a character from the movie Spirited Away written by Hayao Miyazaki. Haku's full name is Nigihayami Kohhakunushi. Haku is Yubaba's apprentice and second in charge at the bathhouse. At the end of the movie you find out that he is the spirit of a river. Although throughout the movie he switches from being a boy thats about 12 years old to a silver dragon character. In the Japanese culture and mythology, there are a lot of associations between a dragon and a river.

In the movie Spirited Away, Haku helps the main character Chihiro Ogino, also known as Sen, find and save her parents who have turned into bigs. This movie is a Japanese classic and has become world famous and translated into many different languages. During the movie, Haku gets hurt and Sen helps him recover by feeding him with a special black ball. Haku was able to recover and help Sen and her parents return back to the real world in one piece.
